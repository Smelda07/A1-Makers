How we work

Project realization process

Every project goes through strict quality control. From the initial CAD design to the final surface finishing, we maintain millimeter precision.

1. Design

We create the design according to customer requirements in 3D CAD software, SolidWorks.

2. Technology

We machine individual parts independently on CNC machines within the required tolerances.

3. Production

We focus on maximum tool functionality, precise dimensions, and proper venting.

4. Materials

We manufacture our tools from aluminum alloy EN AW 5083 or higher-grade alloys.

5. Accessories

We equip the tools with accessories from PK, Stutzer, Stieglbauer, Michel, Kurz, and Heitz.

6. Fasteners

Fasteners and other special mold parts are made from stainless steel.
